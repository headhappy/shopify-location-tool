Update Product Locations

Find a product by barcode, SKU or product name, then update its display, quick-stock and stockroom locations.

Product metafield: custom.display_loc. LOD is saved as a suffix, such as TDC1 (LOD), so the physical display position is never lost.
Variant metafield: stock.location. Use for direct, quick-to-customer stock kept near the shop floor.
Product metafield: custom.location. Use for bulk back stock held in the stockroom.
Blank protection: empty boxes are never written over an existing Shopify location. Only fields that have changed and contain a value are saved.
